Posting Content Packages on PartnerNet

After you've tested your content package on your local Xbox development console, XLAST enables you to post it to PartnerNet for testing. Sending your package to PartnerNet requires your development PC to be connected to the Internet. If it is, click Action in the main menu, and then click Send to Partnernet. XLAST then opens a browser window so that you can use the upload page on xds.xbox.com

Note    It may take up to 24 hours before your package is available on PartnerNet. You will receive an email notification of its availability.

After your content package has been thoroughly tested on PartnerNet and works the way you want it to, you can send it to Microsoft for certification.
