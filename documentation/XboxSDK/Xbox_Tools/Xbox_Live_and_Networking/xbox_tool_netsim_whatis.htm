What is NetSim 2?

Most games for Xbox Live are developed on local area networks. Unfortunately, LANs do not provide a realistic reflection of network gaming across the Internet. It is difficult to tell how gracefully a game for Xbox Live handles the type of real-world problems it might encounter when used on the Internet. The NetSim 2 tool simulates real-world network conditions to enable developers to accurately test their games.

NetSim 2 can simulate limited bandwidth, network latencies, packet loss, and authentication delays. With the NetSim 2 tool's ability to simulate these conditions, you can test your game's ability to communicate from one Xbox to another, and from an Xbox to the Xbox Live servers. This enables you to test your games for compliance with the Xbox Live TCRs.
