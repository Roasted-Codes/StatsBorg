Ping PartnerNet Tool (PingPNet)

The PingPNet tool is used to validate the connection between your Xbox development console and PartnerNet. This tool is preinstalled on your console after you use the recovery disc to update your Xbox System Software. To use this tool, launch it from the XDK Launcher. Once it is activated, you are prompted to press any button to test your connection to PartnerNet. When you are finished viewing the results of the test, pull the left trigger, right trigger, and press the Black button simultaneously to return to the XDK Launcher.
