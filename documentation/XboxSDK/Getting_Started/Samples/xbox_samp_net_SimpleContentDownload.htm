SimpleContentDownload Sample

The SimpleContentDownload sample demonstrates how to enumerate and download content from the Xbox Live services.

Overview

The sample uses the XOnlineOfferingEnumerate and XOnlineContentInstall functions to enumerate and download content. It also shows how to pump the related online tasks, show progress, and detect errors from the Xbox Live service.
