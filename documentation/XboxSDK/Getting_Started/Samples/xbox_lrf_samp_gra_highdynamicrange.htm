HighDynamicRange Sample

The HighDynamicRange sample demonstrates a technique for simulating high dynamic range. The sample adds a blurred and dimmed version of the scene to the original scene to simulate the saturation and blooming common in a high dynamic range image.

Remarks

Copy the media tree to the target console before running this sample.

The HighDynamicRange sample blurs the original scene in two passes using a four tap vertical Gaussian filter, followed by a four tap horizontal Gaussian filter. The sample scales the filter kernel to dim the scene. The blurring is required to smooth out the blooming.
