EnvelopeGenerator Sample

The EnvelopeGenerator sample demonstrates how to use envelope generators on the Xbox console. The envelope generators allow you to specify the following:

  • Delay—how long from start of the sound before sound is audible
  • Attack—length of the transition from zero volume to max volume
  • Hold—length of time from end of attack to start of decay
  • Decay—length of time to decay from maximum volume to zero volume (but volume only goes down to the sustain level)
  • Sustain—main volume level
  • Release—length of time to go from maximum volume to zero volume for release (but starts at the sustain level)

The envelope graph drawn on the screen will help to visualize the effects of decay and release. Note that when a sound is looping, the envelope affects only the start and end of playback.
