Console LED Color and Blink Patterns

When certain hardware or software errors occur, the Xbox development console blinks its DVD LED in specific patterns. The following table describes the meaning of the patterns.

Pattern Meaning
Red, Orange, Red, Orange (debugger attached) Crash Dump: The console has performed a crash dump.
Flashing Orange Thermal Overheat: The console is too hot. Turn it off immediately.
Flashing Green Normal Use: The DVD is in use.
Green, Orange, Green, Orange AV Pack Missing: No AV pack is installed.
Red Tray Error: A DVD is in the correct position in the DVD tray.
Red, Orange, Red, Orange (no debugger attached) RAM Error: Some of the RAM in the console is no longer functional.
Long Red, Green Hard Disk Error: The console's hard drive may have failed.
Red, Long Green DVD Error: The console's DVD drive is not working properly.
Green, Orange, Red, Orange USB Overload: Too many devices are plugged into the USB port. Remove some of them.
