DirectMusicDoWork

Perform DirectMusic work items.

void DirectMusicDoWork(
    DWORD dwQuantum
);

Parameters

dwQuantum
Amount of time, in milliseconds, before the application expects to call DirectMusicDoWork again. Typically this will equal the video frame rate, presuming DirectMusicDoWork is called once every frame.

Remarks

If your title has specified that no threads be used when the performance was initialized, DirectMusicDoWork must be called periodically (typically once every video frame) by the title to allow DirectMusic to perform necessary processing.
