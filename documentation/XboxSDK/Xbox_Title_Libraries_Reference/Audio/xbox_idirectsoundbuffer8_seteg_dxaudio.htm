IDirectSoundBuffer8::SetEG

Sets one of the two envelope generators on the buffer.

HRESULT SetEG(
    LPCDSENVELOPEDESC pEnvelopeDesc
);

Parameters

pEnvelopeDesc
Sets the amplitude envelope for this voice according to the specified DSENVELOPEDESC structure.

Remarks

The envelope generators (EGs) are used to vary the voice parameters at a sub-audio rate (less than about 23 Hz). There are two EGs per voice. One EG is used to modify the amplitude of the voice (EG1). The second can modify the pitch and/or filter cutoff frequency for the voice (EG2).
