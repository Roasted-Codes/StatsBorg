These are the constant registers. Derived from the D3DRS_TEXTUREFACTOR constant, the constant factor registers are loaded by the application through the IDirect3DDevice8::SetPixelShaderConstant method.

The constant factor registers are read-only. There are at least eight constant factor registers defined for use by a pixel shader, but a maximum of two may be used in any one instruction. The values of the constant registers range from 0 to 1.
