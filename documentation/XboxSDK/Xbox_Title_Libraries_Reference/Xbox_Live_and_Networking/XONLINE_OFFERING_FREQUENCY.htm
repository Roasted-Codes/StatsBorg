XONLINE_OFFERING_FREQUENCY

Specifies how often charges are made for an offering.

typedef enum {
    ONE_TIME_CHARGE = 0,
    MONTHLY,
    QUARTERLY,
    BIANNUALLY,
    ANNUALLY,
} XONLINE_OFFERING_FREQUENCY;

Constants

ONE_TIME_CHARGE
The charge applies one time only.
MONTHLY
The charge occurs monthly.
QUARTERLY
The charge occurs four times a year.
BIANNUALLY
The charge occurs twice a year.
ANNUALLY
The charge occurs once a year.
